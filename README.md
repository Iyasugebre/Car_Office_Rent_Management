# Car Office Rent Management

This project is an Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) system designed to manage branch office rentals, vehicle operations, and utility services within an organization.

It streamlines:
- Contract management  
- Vehicle maintenance tracking  
- Automated notification workflows  

---

## 📌 Features

### 🏢 Branch & Office Rent Management
- Manage branch creation requests  
- Store rental agreements  
- Track contract duration and expiry  

### 📄 Agreement Management
- Agreement approval workflow  
- Renewal, amendment, and termination  
- Automated expiry notifications  

### 🚗 Vehicle Management
- Service and maintenance requests  
- Maintenance history tracking  
- Scheduled service reminders  

### 📅 License & Inspection Tracking
- Track vehicle license (Bolo)  
- Monitor inspection expiry  
- Automated alerts  

### 🔔 Notification System
- Contract expiry alerts  
- Rent payment reminders  
- Vehicle service notifications  

### 💡 Utility Management
- Electricity, water, internet, and telephone tracking  
- Monthly payment reminders  

---

## 🎯 Goal

To provide a centralized system for managing organizational assets, contracts, and operations efficiently.
